Frequently Asked Questions

What are smart physical storage spaces?

Smart storage spaces are physical units equipped with advanced features like climate control, digital access, and security monitoring, designed for safe and convenient storage.

How do I rent a storage space?

Simply visit our Rent a Space page, select the unit size and features you need, and complete the booking process online.

What sizes of storage units are available?

We offer a variety of sizes, from small lockers for personal items to large units suitable for furniture or business inventory.

Are my items secure in your storage spaces?

Yes, all our units feature 24/7 video surveillance, digital locks with personalized access codes, and optional insurance for added peace of mind.

Can I access my storage unit at any time?

Our facilities are accessible 24/7 using your unique digital access credentials.
